## Scope

Latin Mass Companion is designed to:
- work offline after installation
- include the full Ordinary of the Mass
- bundle 2026 Sunday and major-feast coverage
- support Low Mass and Sung Mass

It is not presented as a complete hand missal and does not cover every weekday or celebration.
