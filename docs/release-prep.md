# Latin Mass Companion 1.0 Release Prep

## Positioning
Latin Mass Companion should launch as a `trust-first companion` for the `1962 Mass`, not as a claim to be a complete digital missal.

Core launch promise:
- works offline after install
- bundles one explicit year of `2026` Sunday and major-feast coverage
- keeps the full Ordinary available everywhere
- supports `Low Mass` and `Sung Mass`
- helps newcomers and regular attendees follow without anxiety

## App Store Copy Draft

### Subtitle
Offline 1962 Mass companion

### Promotional text
Follow the 1962 Mass with a calm, offline companion built around clear scope, bundled yearly coverage, and practical guidance for both Low and Sung Mass.

### Description draft
Latin Mass Companion is an offline-first iPhone app for following the 1962 Mass with clarity and restraint.

It is designed for two kinds of users at once: people attending for the first time, and regular attendees who want a clean, date-aware companion without depending on a network connection.

What the app does:
- shows the fixed Ordinary of the Mass in a guided flow
- bundles 2026 Sunday and major-feast coverage for date-specific propers
- lets you choose Low Mass or Sung Mass so the guide leans on the right landmarks
- includes pronunciation help, glossary notes, participation guidance, and a small Gregorian chant primer
- keeps bookmarks and Mass progress on-device

What the app does not claim:
- it is not a complete hand missal
- it does not cover every weekday or every celebration
- it does not invent missing propers outside its bundled coverage

Latin Mass Companion is built to help you stay oriented, recollected, and honest about what the app can actually support.
