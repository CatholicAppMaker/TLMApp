# Latin Mass Companion Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Latin Mass Companion is designed to work offline after installation and to keep your reading progress, bookmarks, and preferences on your device.

## Information We Collect

Latin Mass Companion does not create user accounts, does not ask you to sign in, and does not intentionally collect personal information from you.

The app does not include:
- account creation
- analytics SDKs
- advertising SDKs
- third-party trackers
- in-app purchases
- user-generated content uploads

## Data Stored On Device

To make the app easier to use, Latin Mass Companion stores a small amount of app state locally on your device, such as:
- your selected Mass form
- bookmarked sections
- your most recent place in the guide

This information stays on your device and is not transmitted to us by the app.

## Network Use

Latin Mass Companion is intended to function offline after installation. The bundled Mass content, glossary material, pronunciation guides, and learning notes are included inside the app.

## Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your data.

We do not share personal data with third parties through the app because the app is not designed to collect that data in the first place.

## Children

Latin Mass Companion is a general-reference and worship-aid app and is not directed specifically to children under 13.

## Changes

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted with a revised effective date.
